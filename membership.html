Membership Benefits

Unlock Opportunities, Grow Your Network, Influence Policy

Becoming a NACCIMA member gives your business a competitive edge, access to exclusive resources, and a stronger voice in shaping Nigeria's business landscape.

Benefits

1
Capacity Building
  • Bespoke seminars, workshops, and training tailored to your needs.
  • In-depth analysis of federal legislative developments and their impact on business.
2
Expand Your Network
  • Exclusive access to local and international B2B matchmaking opportunities.
  • Real-time data on economic and social issues affecting business and the economy.
  • Easy access to suppliers and buyers through the Market Access platform.
3
Advocacy & Representation
  • Representation on tariff negotiations and lobbying to achieve your objectives.
  • Platform to dialogue with government on business-related policies.
  • Nomination to serve on governmental boards, commissions, and committees.
4
Networking & Global Opportunities
  • Access to exclusive international B2B meetings.
  • Opportunities to participate in trade missions to key markets worldwide.
  • Invitations to local and international exhibitions and trade fairs.
5
Member Privileges
  • Discounted rates for Certificates of Origin (COI) for exports.
  • Assistance for exporters and buyers through visa procurement.
Corporate Membership

Corporate Membership is open to businesses of all sizes, ranging from small enterprises to large corporations. As a Corporate Member, you will gain access to several benefits and opportunities, including participation in industry-specific forums, networking events, trade missions, and policy advocacy initiatives. You will also have the chance to contribute to shaping local and national policies that directly impact your industry.

Bi-lateral Membership

Bi-lateral Membership is designed for chambers of commerce, industry, mines, and agriculture from other countries that wish to establish a collaborative relationship. As a Bi-lateral Member, you'll enjoy opportunities for trade missions, networking, and exchange programs with like-minded organizations across Nigeria and beyond.

Chambers Membership

Chamber Membership is dedicated to businesses, professional associations, and trade groups seeking to strengthen their presence and influence within the private sector. This membership provides access to exclusive events, capacity-building sessions, and advocacy platforms to help you establish strategic partnerships and maximize new business opportunities.

How to Join

Joining NACCIMA means becoming part of a national network of business leaders, entrepreneurs, and innovators working together to shape Nigeria's economic future. Follow these simple steps to become a member today.

Step 1

Complete the Application Form

Fill out our membership form online or download a copy. This captures basic details about your business and ensures we place you in the right category.

Step 2

Submit Your Documents

Provide supporting documents such as CAC Registration and Tax ID. These help verify your business and confirm eligibility.

Step 3

Pay Membership Dues

Membership fees depend on your category. Once reviewed, you'll receive payment instructions with secure options for settlement.

Step 4

Confirmation & Welcome

After approval, you'll receive your Membership Certificate and login details for the Members' Portal. From here, you can access events, opportunities, and benefits.
